Welcome to Pure Meat!

Getting started with our fresh meat delivery service is easy. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Visit Our Website: Go to perumeats.in to access our services
  2. Create Account: Sign up with your email or phone number
  3. Verify: Confirm your account through email or SMS
  4. Set Preferences: Add your delivery address and meat preferences
  5. Start Shopping: Browse our fresh meat selection and place your first order

How to Place an Order

Ordering fresh, quality meat has never been easier:

  1. Browse Categories: Explore beef, chicken, pork, lamb, and specialty cuts
  2. Select Products: Choose from various cuts, weights, and preparation methods
  3. Add to Cart: Specify quantities and any special instructions
  4. Review Order: Check your items and apply any promo codes
  5. Checkout: Select delivery time and complete payment
  6. Track: Monitor your order status in real-time

Delivery Information

We ensure your meat arrives fresh and on time:

  • Delivery Hours: 8 AM - 8 PM, Monday to Saturday
  • Same-Day Delivery: Orders placed before 2 PM
  • Real-Time Tracking: Track your order from preparation to delivery
  • Temperature Control: Special packaging maintains freshness
  • Quality Guarantee: 100% satisfaction or your money back

Payment Options

All payments are securely processed through Razorpay, India's leading payment gateway:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express
  • UPI: Instant payments through UPI apps (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, etc.)
  • Net Banking: Direct bank account payments
  • Digital Wallets: PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay
  • Cash on Delivery: Pay when you receive your order
  • Bank Transfer: Direct bank payments for large orders

Security: Razorpay ensures all transactions are protected with 256-bit SSL encryption and PCI DSS compliance.

Managing Your Account

Take control of your account and preferences:

  • Profile Settings: Update personal information and preferences
  • Address Book: Save multiple delivery addresses
  • Order History: View past orders and reorder favorites
  • Notifications: Customize alerts for orders and promotions
  • Payment Methods: Save cards for faster checkout
